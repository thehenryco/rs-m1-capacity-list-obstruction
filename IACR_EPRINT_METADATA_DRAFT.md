Title: A Capacity-Radius List-Decoding Obstruction for the m=1 Specialization of Reed-Solomon Codes
Author: Jerod Harbor
Repository: https://github.com/thehenryco/rs-m1-capacity-list-obstruction
Status: TO_BE_SUBMITTED_BY_AUTHOR

Abstract: This note gives an m=1 ordinary-list specialization for Reed-Solomon capacity-radius list size. It proves exact list size binom(n,k) at radius n-k under a generic received-word condition and derives threshold failure for binom(n,k+1) < q < 2^128 binom(n,k).
